Class: Knife

Attribute:
knife_type (string)
is_sharp (boolean)
knife_length (float, inches)
owner_name (string)
is_dirty (boolean)

Methods:
chop (use a short movement to cut; could modify attribute is_sharp and is_clean)
change_owner (change of ownership; would modify attribute owner_name)
be_cleaned (would modify attribute is_clean)
be_sharpened (would modify attribute is_sharp, could modify attribute is_clean)
